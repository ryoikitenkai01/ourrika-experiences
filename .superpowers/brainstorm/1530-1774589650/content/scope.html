What kind of UI rework?

This shapes everything — scope, effort, and which pages we touch first.

A

Complete visual overhaul

New aesthetic direction — typography, color application, spacing system, component style. The bones stay, everything visual changes.

B

Targeted page improvements

Specific pages or sections feel off. Fix those, leave the rest. Surgical, lower risk, faster to ship.

C

Conversion-focused restructure

Layout and hierarchy changes to drive more bookings — better CTAs, stronger trust signals, clearer flow from landing to WhatsApp.

D

Mobile-first redesign

Current site is desktop-biased. Rebuild the mobile experience — sticky bars, touch targets, simplified layouts for small screens.
